## 1.创建Springboot项目
pom依赖：
```java
        <dependency>
            <groupId>org.springframework.boot</groupId>
            <artifactId>spring-boot-starter-web</artifactId>
        </dependency>

        <dependency>
            <groupId>com.taobao.arthas</groupId>
            <artifactId>arthas-spring-boot-starter</artifactId>
            <version>3.3.6</version>
        </dependency>
```
